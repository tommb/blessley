I'm Tom Blessley, a product designer based in London, currently working at Bark building a marketplace for services. Please check out some of my design work over here.

I'm so lucky to have travelled and explored so many cities across Europe over the past 20 years, and it's something I hope to continue in a post pandemic world. These photos are naive, captured over a few days spent being curious, exploring a city's history, culture, and presence. I hope they stand as testament to how much I've enjoyed visiting these cities, and the people I spent time wondering in them with.
